Interests and Hobbies

Music
I am an enthusiastic musician proficient in a variety of instruments. I have completed the highest level (Grade 10) in both piano and vocal music. Additionally, I am skilled in playing the bamboo flute, harmonica, and classical guitar.
Psychology
As a psychology enthusiast, I have undergone systematic training in psychological counseling. I understand various schools of thought including psychoanalysis, psychodynamics, and humanism, with a particular affinity for Adlerian psychology.
Debate
My analytical mindset, honed through academic endeavors and enriched by my experience in debate, equips me to tackle problems with depth and critical thinking. I enjoy delving into different perspectives, considering individuals' emotions and motivations.
Puzzle Games
I am fond of various puzzle games, including Sudoku, Go, and brain teasers. These games challenge my problem-solving skills and provide a fun and engaging way to keep my mind active.
Reading
I have a particular fondness for literary works, especially "Les Misérables" and "Dream of the Red Chamber," which are profound reflections of their respective times and cultures.
Photography
I have a keen eye for the beauty surrounding us and enjoy capturing it through photography. I often photograph portraits for friends, immortalizing our shared memories and experiences.
